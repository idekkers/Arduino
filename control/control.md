# Arduino code for hydrophonics control system
the system recieves water and environmental data - 
- water PH
- water conductivity
- water temp
- air temp
- air humidity
- barometric preasure
- light intensity

and uses it to operate relays to strat and stop
- pumps for nutrients, acid and base
- fans
- lights
- aircon
- heaters
- co2 pumps
